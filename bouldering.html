Bouldering Team

Preseason

Preseason week will be held 24th-28th September. The training will include:

  • Technique sessions at the DCC
  • Core sessions
  • An in-house bouldering competition
  • A trip to a local crag
  • Slacklining Trip
This event is open to anyone considering competing throughout the year whether that’s for BUCS or our friendly competitions. Don’t worry if your accommodation doesn’t start until the next week, current members can offer spare rooms to stay in. Email us mountaineering.club@dur.ac.uk ASAP if you are interested.

Competitions

DUMC have an annual BUCS competition in mid-February. To compete in this event members are selected by the bouldering captain. Our bouldering captain for 2018/19 is Dan Chadfield, aka Strong Dan (due to the ridiculous amount of Dans in our club and the size of his biceps). Please note, if you are called Dan and wish to join the club you will get a nickname because there really are too many of you. If you have any questions about joining the team or Preseason you can email Strong Dan on the club email mountaineering.club@dur.ac.uk . Throughout the year there are other friendly competitions that we encourage any member to take part in. These took place once a month for October, November, December and February. In April we started a friendly competition at the Valley in Newcastle, a fantastic event for all abilities with food provided and freebies!
