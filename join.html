How to join

So you want to be one of those climber people? Fantastic! Here's how to join the club:

  1. Join the "DUMC Forum" on Facebook to stay up to date with what we're doing.
  2. Come and find our stall at the fresher's fair. We'll be there to chat about what we do and you'll be able to leave your email to join our mailing list.
  3. Come to our Freshers' talk on 08/10/18 at 18:30 in CG85
