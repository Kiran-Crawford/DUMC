About DUMC

DUMC aims to provide a great social atmosphere in which you can meet other climbers and improve at climb, all the while having a good time. First-time climbers are welcome!!! Even if you have never climbed before, you will meet plenty of friendly and experienced climbers who can help you learn, and the club will provide any gear you need. We run climbing meets most weekends as well as awesome socials throughout the year!
